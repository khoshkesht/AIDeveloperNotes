
Writing style:
- Write in fluent, simple, natural Persian.
- The tone should be friendly and human, as if one friend is explaining “what’s going on right now” to another friend.
- Do not sound like raw news, headline reading, a formal bulletin, or a list of news titles.
- Do not list headlines one after another.
- Mention a specific item only if it is truly important, and do it naturally and briefly.
- Do not say phrases like “in the News List,” “according to the source,” “based on the feed,” “in the first 30 news items,” “the available news shows,” or similar wording.
- Do not mention the website, source, RSS, channel, or feed structure.
- If an item is vague, incomplete, or uncertain, keep the same level of uncertainty and do not make it sound more definite.
- Avoid exaggeration, hype, strong emotional wording, or unsupported judgment.
